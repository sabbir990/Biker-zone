Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most important things I should know about riding a bike?

Safety First! Always obey the rules of the road. Obey all traffic signals, signs, and laws. Get in the mindset of “driving” your bike—not just “riding” your bike. This will help you be a more focused and legally compliant bike rider.

How can I tell if my helmet is old and I need a new one?

Motorcycle helmets are one of the most essential riding safety gears that you cannot ignore if you value your health and wellness. In most cases, people replace their motorcycle helmets earlier than they need to, but there are also helmets that are continuously put in use even long after they have passed their primes.

What rules should I follow when riding my bike?

Adjust Your Bicycle to Fit. Stand over your bicycle. There should be 1 to 2 inches between you and the top tube (bar) if using a road bike and 3 to 4 inches if a mountain bicycle. The seat should be level front to back.
